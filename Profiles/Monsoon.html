Rowan (Monsoon)

-Gemini incarnate-

You could claim to be prepared for everything and will still end up surprised when it comes to Rowan.
Being the literal definition of a Gemini, she can be the most energetic person you have ever seen, but once her social batteries run out,
she will enter the “Rowan-coma”, which can easily be identified by her being completely silent all of a sudden and
everyone around her telling her to go to sleep.
Naturally witty and entertaining, Rowan can fill a room with energy and good vibes by just being there.
